About me

Technology Roots: My journey began in the realm of technology, where I honed my skills in HTML5, CSS, PHP, SQL, and C#. These foundations instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, precision, and an unrelenting passion for problem-solving.

Diverse Academic Pursuits: Driven by a deep-seated curiosity and personal aspirations, I transitioned into the world of health sciences. My academic journey saw me earn three postgraduate degrees and eventually culminated in a doctorate in health sciences. My experiences as a clinical practitioner and a dedicated professor and researcher allowed me to develop a comprehensive understanding of the healthcare landscape.

Prolific Scholar and Author: I am proud to share that I have authored two books and contributed to over 25 papers in international scientific journals. My dedication to fostering the next generation of healthcare professionals has been exemplified through my role as a mentor and consultant to numerous students, masters, and doctoral candidates. My expertise in statistical analysis has helped shape the future of health sciences research for many individuals and myself.

The Convergence of Fields: Recognizing the immense potential at the intersection of machine learning and health sciences, I embarked on a new adventure. I mastered Python and delved into data analysis and visualization techniques. Simultaneously, I initiated a bachelor's degree in Computer Science to deepen my understanding of the computational side of things.

Innovative Portfolio: Before even embarking on my bachelor's degree, I crafted a remarkable portfolio that showcases my skills and knowledge. My website and the machine learning models I've developed serve as a testament to my commitment to excellence. These tools are readily available for your projects, demonstrating my willingness to share and contribute to your initiatives.

Your Ideal Collaborator: I am now at a point where I can offer an unparalleled blend of technological acumen, health sciences expertise, and data-driven insights. I am eagerly looking for opportunities to apply my diverse skill set in a professional capacity. Whether it's consulting, research collaboration, or full-time employment, I am fully committed to adding value to your organization.

Intrigued? Explore my portfolio and see how my journey has come full circle, uniting technology and health sciences through the power of data and machine learning. I invite you to connect with me, as I am ready to embark on the next chapter of my remarkable journey and contribute to your team's success. Together, we can create something truly extraordinary.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the exciting possibilities that lie ahead!
